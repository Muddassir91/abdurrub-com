Operations

Building and owning marketing operations.

Most marketing teams ship fast and forget faster. Messaging lives in Slack threads, testimonials live in one person's head, and every campaign starts from a blank page.

At Passion.io I built the opposite. A messaging system the whole acquisition team opens before they write a single line.

01

Organized by what the info does, not where it came from.

Six Notion databases. Pain, objection, benefit, persona, outcome, output. Each with a clear role in the writing process.

02

Any writer can pull sentences the customer would actually say, rather than guessing.

03

One output database turns the rest into shipped work.

Angles pulls from the other five to produce ready-to-ship hooks, headlines, and openings.
