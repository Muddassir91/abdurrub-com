Passion.io case-study cluster

Not one case study. A full lifecycle operating surface.

The Passion.io work covered acquisition emails, launch sequences, nurture revenue, webinar follow-up, customer research, and the marketing systems underneath the writing.

Passion.ioLifecycle revenue

Built the email work behind $2.4M+ in attributed revenue.

Problem: A fast-moving SaaS team needed email to support launches, webinars, seasonal campaigns, nurture, and product education without turning every send into isolated copy.

Owned: Email copy, messaging angles, launch sequences, nurture content, offer framing, and recurring updates from sales-call and webinar feedback.

Result: $547K in 2023, $1.13M in 2024, and more than $2.4M across the engagement.

Passion.ioBlack Friday

Drove $256K in email revenue in a 30-day Black Friday window.

Problem: Black Friday needed urgency, offer clarity, repeated proof, and enough variation to keep the list responsive across the campaign.

Owned: 11 emails across seven days, subject-line framing, offer reminders, bonus-stack repetition, and direct response urgency.

Result: $256,094 from email in 30 days. The broader BFCM program reached $380K total revenue.

Passion.ioWebinar follow-up

Built the sequences that produced the largest email revenue line.

Problem: Live workshops and webinars created buying intent, but the follow-up layer had to carry objections, urgency, product value, and sales-team learnings.

Owned: Follow-up email structure, objection-led messaging, founder-workshop feedback extraction, and recurring sequence refreshes.

Result: Automated webinar follow-ups became the biggest email revenue source in the Passion.io lifecycle program.

Passion.ioNurture

Grew pure nurture revenue on the same list.

Problem: The newsletter needed to become a revenue channel, not just a weekly send.

Owned: Nurture emails, educational angles, product proof, objections, and reusable messaging from customer research.

Result: Newsletter email revenue grew 3.5x from 2023 to 2025.

Passion.ioResearch system

Turned sales calls, support tickets, and workshop notes into marketing inputs.

Problem: A growing team needed fresh messaging inputs that reflected the market, not recycled internal claims.

Owned: Voice-of-customer inputs, sales-call takeaways, webinar notes, objection logs, persona work, and Notion-backed marketing databases.

Result: New copywriters and collaborators had a source of truth for pain points, objections, personas, offers, hooks, and benefits.

Osum/Awesome.comLaunch email

Supported welcome and cart abandonment emails for a SaaS launch.

Problem: New users and high-intent visitors needed sharper email touchpoints around entry, education, and abandoned purchase intent.

Owned: Launch welcome emails and cart abandonment copy built around clearer product value and action prompts.

Result: A cleaner lifecycle entry point for launch traffic and intent recovery. Public revenue metrics are not available for this engagement.
