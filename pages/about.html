Message from Muddassir Abdur Rub

Who am I, and why you should trust me with your marketing.

Muddassir
Direct Response Marketer

In 2022, I was in $5,000+ of debt, with no real plan to pay it back.

Which might not seem a lot in USD, but it's more than what 80% of Pakistan's population makes in a year. It could've taken me a lifetime to pay it back with a traditional job.

By every external measure I was supposed to have made it:

The first doctor in my family.

Cleared Pakistan's most competitive civil services exam on the first attempt.

I did most things right. It wasn't enough. Or so I thought.

So I chose to start a freelance marketing service online, which was scary, because ~90% of my colleagues from med school went on to Fellowships. I was the only one not following the herd.

Three years later, I've helped move over $10 million in client revenue, $2.4 million of it for one company alone, and built marketing systems for founders, brands, and operators who now treat retention the way I do: as the cheapest growth channel they already own. And yes, I paid off all that debt.

If I could explain my entire life in one sentence, it would be this:

I can't do average.

And I bring the same don't-settle-for-average philosophy to the work.

My mission is to help 1,000 new businesses scale past $100K. Starting a business has become easier than ever in the age of AI. Growing and scaling one past $100K is about to become harder than ever. That's the gap I want to help founders close.

If you've stalled past $100K, or you're sitting on a list you barely email, I'd like to be useful.
