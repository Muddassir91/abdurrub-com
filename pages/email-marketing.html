Process

Four moves. Ninety days.

01

Audit the leak

Start with the data already in the platform. Open rate, click depth, time-to-second-purchase, list health. Find the leak before fixing anything.

02

Pull the language

Reviews, support tickets, sales calls, Reddit threads. Real words, sorted into desires, fears, and objections. The voice gets built from this, not from a brand doc.

03

Build the cycle

Welcome, post-purchase, win-back, weekly broadcast. Hand-written first cycle, sent in real time, against a real list, with a real number to beat.

04

Scaffold for the team

Templates, voice guide, segmentation logic, calendar. Everything the in-house team needs to keep the engine running without the consultant in the loop.

Common questions

The short answers.

What does the engagement look like?
A two-week audit, then a 30 to 90 day rebuild of the highest-leverage flows. After that, ongoing optimization or a clean handoff to the in-house team.
Which platforms do you work in?
Klaviyo, Customer.io, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp, ConvertKit, HubSpot. Platform follows the strategy, not the other way around.
Do you write the emails or just plan them?
Both. The first cycle is hand-written so the system has a real voice baseline. After that, the team scales on the same scaffold.
What if the list is small or cold?
Small lists are easier to fix than people think. Cold lists need a re-engagement sequence first, and the rules are different. Both are normal starting points.
