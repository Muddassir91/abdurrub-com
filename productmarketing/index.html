The product context

Passion.io shipped two product lines while I was there

Most of the work below maps to one of these. Calling them out so the case studies make sense.

Platform

Passion.io

The original product. A no-code app builder for coaches and creators to package their courses, communities, and coaching into a branded mobile and web app. Existed before I joined. Email and lifecycle revenue ran here. Black Friday and the $1.115M webinar follow-up sequences both lived on this product line.

Launched mid-tenure

YouAI

An AI coach product that did not exist when I joined. Part of the team that took it from internal feature to live launch funnel. Most of the launch positioning, customer research, and messaging-system work below was in service of this product line.
