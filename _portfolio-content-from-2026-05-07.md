## Customer research land

Tools and method (your own words, organized for the portfolio):

UAI customer research had two streams.

Stream one: sales call analysis. The sales team ran 20+ discovery and sales calls with ICPs. You sat on those calls, recorded transcripts, and extracted three layers from each:
- Voice-of-customer language (verbatim)
- Tonality and psychology read across three signals: facial expression patterns, tone of voice, language they used to describe pain, outcomes, and objections
- Segment patterns that grouped customers into three avatars

Stream two: open-source customer research. You expanded sample size by going into Reddit, native forums, social media, and Google search to find similar avatar profiles outside the sales calls. You extracted pain language, objections, and limiting beliefs and added them to the customer avatar database.

The whole thing lived in Notion as a structured database. Each avatar had: pain points (with quotes), desired outcomes (with quotes), objections (with quotes), and limiting beliefs (with quotes).

For Passion.io: similar systems but research was sourced through the support ticket Slack channel and a recurring two-question email survey to the audience. The same database structure applied.

## Specific moment / research finding

The 100K-to-10M outcome promise insight.

Observation: the UAI outcome promise was a single jump from 100K to 10M, while the rest of the market segmented their promises by revenue band. Top operators in the space were promising up to 1M to brands at 300K-500K, and 10M only to brands already at 3M+. UAI was promising the entire range to a single avatar.

You proposed sub-segmenting the customer base by revenue band:
- 100K to 300K
- 300K to 1M
- 1M to 3M
- 3M+

Each band had distinct problems, distinct desired outcomes, and distinct identity language around freedom, ownership, and entrepreneurship.

The team did not hard disqualify the lower bands; sales suggested downselling Passion.io to those who were not a UAI fit. Later, this was systematized into a downsell flow.

What to share on the portfolio:
- The observation (the gap between the market's segmented promises and UAI's single-jump promise).
- The recommended sub-segmentation by revenue band.
- The downsell idea that came out of the conversation.

What to skip:
